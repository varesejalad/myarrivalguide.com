Important reminders
01
Apply for your second year visa at least 6 weeks before expiry. Don't wait until the last week — processing takes time and you want buffer.
02
Keep every payslip from your 88 days jobs. Scan them the day you get them. You'll need to prove every day when you apply for your second year.
03
Check the postcode qualifies before you start the job. Not all regional postcodes count. Verify on the Home Affairs website first — one wrong postcode and those weeks don't count.
04
Set up superannuation with your first employer. Your employer pays 11% of your wages into super automatically. You claim it all back when you leave Australia. Don't forget this — it's free money.
05
The 88 days don't need to be consecutive. You can work at 3 different farms in different months and add them up. Just keep proper records for each.
